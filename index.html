About Zanzibaba

Based in the beautiful coastal village of Paje in Zanzibar, Tanzania, Zanzibaba is a diversified business group committed to excellence across multiple industries.

Our mission is to provide professional services and quality products while contributing to the sustainable development of Zanzibar and improving the lives of our community.

With our deep local knowledge and global perspectives, we bridge traditional values with modern innovations to create meaningful solutions for our clients and customers.
